Michael Jordan Extra Info

Michael Jeffrey Jordan, also known by his initials MJ, is a former professional basketball player and american businessman. He is the owner and chairman if the Charlotte Hornets of the National Basketball Association or the NBA and of 23XI racing in the NASCAR Cup Series.

  • School: Univeristy of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
  • Career start: 1984, 1994, 1995, 2001
  • Career end: 1993, March 1995, 1998, 2003
  • Position: Shooting guard, Small forward, Outfielder
  • NBA draft: 1984 (Round: 1/Pick: 3)
